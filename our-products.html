Bahia de Paola

Brazilian Farm

Light: A complex blend, this roast offers notes of honey and lemon with a smooth earthy taste.  Ease into the morning with delight. 
Med: A reserved middle of the road type, this blend is made to pair with food. Full of nutty tones that are rounded out with light orange and toffee accents. Highly drinkable. 
Dark: Bring on the night! Easily the darkest of the dark, this is a robust blend with full bodied smoky flavor punctuated with hints of cherry and chocolate. Don’t let it intimidate you, it’s very tasty.

Huila Valdez

Colombian Farm

Light: Our first and most popular blend. Silky smooth with cane sugar sweetness and traces of tropical fruit, this will surely become a favorite for you as well. 
Med: Robust body with blueberry, pomegranate, and winey acidity - this blend is full of whimsical surprises. Complex and comforting, this is the blend to impress your friends and family. 
Dark: This blend is not shy! Mellow smoke meets bitter fruit flavors rounded out with hints of dark cocoa. Deep, bold, and ready for anything the day throws at you. Pairs well with desserts and late nights.

Santiago Alto

Chilean Farm

Light: A delicious sweet blend full of complexity and life. Earthy malt tones accentuated by hints of molasses, caramel, and a light nutty bite. Paired with a full breakfast or by itself, you don’t want to miss out on this one. 
Med: There’s nothing average about this roast. Ultra smooth with complex highlights or bourbon, vanilla, and smoky citrus. This blend will remind you of why you love coffee in the first place. 
Dark: Dip your toe in the dark side. This is a great beginners dark roast - highly approachable with a toasted cocoa flavor capped by light floral notes. Embrace the dark side.
